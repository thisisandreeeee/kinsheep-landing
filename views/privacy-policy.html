Privacy Policy

Updated 15 July 2015

This privacy policy (the “Policy”) explains the manner in which we, Kinsheep Pte Ltd (“Kinsheep”), maintain and collect personal information (“Information”) about you. This Policy applies to Information we collect via our applications and websites.

By providing us with Information, you accept our Policy as indicated below.

1. When do we collect Information from you?

We collect Information from you when you make an inquiry through our contact form or directly through our designated email. Moreover, we collect Information from you when you register an account with us and provide us with Information through our electronic registration forms on our websites and applications.

2. How do we collect Information from you?

By filling our electronic registration form on our websites and/or applications, you directly provide us with most of the Information we collect. Also, you provide us with Information when you use our platforms on our websites and applications, when you contact us directly through our designated email or our contact form, and when you respond to newsletters and/or promotions. Additionally, indirect collection of information includes use of cookies (see “Cookies” below); device properties, including, but not limited to Internet Protocol (IP) address, unique device identifier and Media Access Control (“MAC”) or other non-persistent or persistent identifier (“Device ID”); device operating software and firmware; mobile phone carrier; geographical data, including, but not limited to zip code coarse location and area code; stock and/or word searches made on our Analyse platform; trades made on our Analyze and/or Trade platform; word additions and removals conducted on our platforms; time spent on our platforms; achievements; and other non-personal data deemed reasonably required by us to enhance our websites and applications.

If you choose to use Kinsheep’s referral system or a similar service, we may store your friend’s information, including, but not limited to your friend’s contact details and name that you provide to us.

3. How do we use the Information collected?

We use the Information we collect from you in a number of ways:
• to deal with your inquiries done through our contact form and/or directly through our designated email
• to send you newsletters and marketing information about our services and products, but only as permitted by the law or as requested by you
• for internal data analysis and research so we can improve our websites and applications
• to send administrative emails, including, but not limited to when you forget your password and/or username
• to detect and prevent fraud and abuse of our websites and applications
• to reasonably operate our websites and applications

4. How do we protect your Information?

We take precautions to protect your information. When you submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and offline.

Wherever we collect sensitive information (such as credit card data), that information is encrypted and transmitted to us in a secure way. You can verify this by looking for a closed lock icon at the bottom of your web browser, or looking for "https" at the beginning of the address of the web page.

While we use encryption to protect sensitive information transmitted online, we also protect your information offline. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job (for example, billing or customer service) are granted access to personally identifiable information. The computers/servers in which we store personally identifiable information are kept in a secure environment. Our marketing partners, affiliated companies and other organisations that work for us may have access to the Information.

In certain circumstances, we may disclose the Information if required by the law or defend others or ourselves against illegal or harmful activities.

In the scenario where our business is sold or restructured, the Information may be transferred as part of the business.

5. Cookies

Our websites and applications may contain cookies. Cookies are text files placed on your computer that do not contain any personally identifiable information but they help us improve your access to our site and identify repeat visitors to our site. For instance, when we use a cookie to identify you, you would not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on our site. Cookies can also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our site.

6. Links

Our websites and applications may contain links other websites and applications. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personally identifiable information.

7. Accessing and updating your Information

Subject to the exceptions referred to in section 21(2) of the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (No. 26 of 2012) of Singapore (“PDPA”), you have the right to request your Information.

If you would like your Information, please contact us through our contact form with the Header “Privacy Policy Information.” We may charge an administrative fee to provide your Information.

We continually ensure your Information is accurate and up-to-date. If any Information you have given to Kinsheep changes, please update them through your profile panel or send an email to us through our contact form.

8. Changes to Policy

Our Policy may change from time to time and all updates will be posted on this page. If you feel that we are not abiding by this Policy, you should contact us immediately via our contact page with the header “Privacy Policy Issues”.

9. Questions

Should you have any questions regarding our Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us using our contact form with the header “Privacy Policy Questions”.
